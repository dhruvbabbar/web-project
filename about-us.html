Rahee group is a leading integrated railroad company based in India, operating in the field of Rail Tracks and Bridges for over sixty years. Established in 1948, the group specializes in Construction of Bridges, Track Installations, Flash Butt Welding of Rails, Turnout Systems, Fastening Systems, other Permanent Way Products and Bridge Products.

Rahee is proud to be associated with Indian Railways, one of the largest state owned railway network in the world, producing premium products enhancing safe rail journeys at every stage. The group has several plants in India, manufacturing various track products for Railways. The plants are spread across the Eastern region of India, Kolkata and Southern region of India, Hyderabad. In addition, there are several infrastructure projects executed and under execution across India creating a pan India presence for the group.

Rahee’s consistent endeavour has led the company to be rewarded and recognized as the “Best upcoming company in the Infrastructure Segment” along with “Most Innovative Enterprise” by CNBCTV18in 2013.

Supplementing Rahee Group’s expertise is a wealth of international experience in the form of its association with Pandrol Limited, UK for supply of resilient fastenings and other technologically advanced solutions for modern Metros/Railways. The collaboration has strengthened Rahee’s position as a railway track leader in India.

Featuring quality materials, providing experienced and reliable services; Rahee satisfies the requirements with regards to innovation, cost effectiveness and performance. Quality and on time delivery are our core values and we strive to live by our motto “Quality First, Speed always”. These values give Rahee an edge and are a great source of convenience for its clients.

Rahee Infratech Ltd. (RIL)
‍Established in 1948, Rahee Infratech Ltd. is the flagship company of the Rahee Group. The company was established as a partnership firm under the name and style of Ramchander Heeralall; to manufacture Railway Track Products. It has come a long way since and today the company excels in Civil Construction, Bridge Works, Track Works, Steel Fabrication, Mobile Flash Butt Welding and manufacturing of engineering components for Railways.

Rahee Track Technologies Pvt. Ltd. (RTT)
‍RTT is involved in manufacturing of Railway turnouts and permanent way products. Rahee started its Turnout operations in 1994. In the year 2006 a new state of the art Turnout unit was established at Jangalpur near Kolkata to improve and consolidate the production base and infuse new technology into the manufacturing processes. Today, RTT owns the most modern plant in the country to manufacture and export Switches, Crossings, Turnouts, Improved Switch Expansion Joints, Switch Setting Devices, Glued Insulated Joints and Steel Sleepers.

Pandrol Rahee Technologies Pvt. Ltd. (PRT)
‍PRT is a joint venture initiative between Pandrol UK Ltd. and Rahee Infratech Ltd. The company was formed in 2005, to market Pandrol Fastenings in India. PRT concentrates exclusively on manufacturing and supply of Pandrol Products for High Speed, Heavy Haul and Metro Rail applications in India. PRT has established the most modern state of the art manufacturing facility in India for production of clips in the state of Telangana.
